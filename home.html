Solution

Financial Solutions and Thematic Programs to Support Fund

At Boukaseed, we offer a range of financial solutions and thematic programs designed to support the growth and success of startups and small businesses.

Discover tailored funding options and expert financial support to help your mission-driven startup thrive.

CREDIT QUARANTEES

Secure the financing you need with our Credit Guarantees. These guarantees provide the security that lenders require, making it easier for your startup to obtain loans and other forms of credit. Our Credit Guarantees are designed to reduce the risk for lenders, ensuring that you can access the necessary funds to grow your business without undue financial strain.

FACTORING

Improve your cash flow with our Factoring services. Factoring allows you to convert your receivables into immediate cash, providing the liquidity you need to manage your operations effectively. By selling your invoices to a third party at a discount, you can receive funds quickly and maintain a healthy cash flow, ensuring that your business remains agile and responsive.

EQUITY/DEPTS

Manage your financial obligations efficiently with our Debts/ Equity solutions. We offer strategic advice and support to help you navigate your debt landscape, whether you're consolidating existing loans or seeking new financing. Our team works with you to create a sustainable debt / Equity management plan that aligns with your business objectives and financial capabilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Boukaseed is a platform developed by Le Boukarou to help mission-driven entrepreneurs raise funds from investors for their businesses.

Investors are individuals or entities that provide capital to businesses in exchange for equity, debt, or other returns. They support startups by funding their growth and development.

A startup is a young company founded to develop a unique product or service and bring it to market. Startups are characterized by innovation, high growth potential, and the need for significant investment.

To be eligible on Boukaseed, your startup should be innovative, mission-driven, scalable, in the early stages of development, and have a well-developed business plan.

You can get help by contacting our support team via our website or email, scheduling a consultation with our advisors, accessing our resources, or joining our community of entrepreneurs and investors.
